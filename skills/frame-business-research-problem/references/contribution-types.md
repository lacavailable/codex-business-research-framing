# Contribution-Type Routing

Identify contribution type after domain and before drafting contribution prose.
Use only the relevant logic.

## Analytical mechanism contribution

Explain the mechanism or interaction, comparative statics, any
counterintuitive result, and the bounded change in understanding.

## Formulation contribution

Explain equivalence, relaxation strength, structural reformulation,
computational consequence, the unchanged true optimum, and any operational
pathway. Do not translate a formulation result into a generic managerial claim.

## Algorithmic contribution

Distinguish solution quality, runtime, guarantee, scale, deadline, and exact or
approximate status. Keep benchmark conditions visible.

## Empirical causal contribution

Identify treatment, identification strategy, counterfactual, measured outcome,
population, and external-validity boundary. Do not use this route for a
stylized model.

## IS design or governance contribution

Identify the artifact or mechanism, information, incentives, behavior,
organizational problem, and implementation boundary. Preserve strategic
behavior when it is central.

## Policy or mechanism-design contribution

Identify actors, incentives, strategic response, information asymmetry,
enforcement, welfare components, and the policy trade-off. Do not substitute a
private objective for welfare.
