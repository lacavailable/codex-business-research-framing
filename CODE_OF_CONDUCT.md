# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We commit to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

Be constructive, distinguish evidence from inference, critique ideas rather than people, respect privacy and intellectual property, and accept good-faith correction.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, deliberate disclosure of private information, plagiarism, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to the maintainers using the contact method in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to protect the community. Reports will be handled as confidentially and promptly as practical.
