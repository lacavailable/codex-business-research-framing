# Third-party notices

Version v0.2.0-beta bundles no third-party full text, PDF, HTML snapshot,
screenshot, article excerpt, publisher diagram, or source-license artifact.
The literature infrastructure contains bibliographic metadata and original
paraphrased notes only. Copyright in cited works remains with the respective
rights holders.

No inclusion in the literature manifest implies affiliation, endorsement, or
permission to redistribute the cited work. Access status and licensing status
are recorded separately and conservatively. See
`literature/review-protocol.md` for the verification policy.
