# Expert calibration protocol

Recruit at least two qualified reviewers per domain. Qualification evidence and identities remain private; public reviewer IDs are pseudonymous and cannot be linked back through committed files. Automated agents are excluded from expert counts.

Reviewers independently record passage function, dimension applicability, raw scores, rankings, fidelity hard failures, concise rationales, confidence, and evidence spans. Publication status and outlet are hidden. Two reviewers assess every accepted passage. Disagreements are retained, not silently averaged away.

Experts also verify semantic equivalence between each source passage and its paraphrase. A paraphrase that changes actor, timing, information, objective, mechanism, boundary, or claim strength is rejected. Sanitized public exports remove passage text, evidence spans, private rationales, qualifications, and identity links.
