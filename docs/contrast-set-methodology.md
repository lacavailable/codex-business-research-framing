# Contrast-set methodology

Each expert-confirmed positive source yields a minimally anonymized passage, an independently written faithful paraphrase, and ten length-controlled one-construct variants: actor, timing, information, objective, mechanism, trade-off, unsupported hype, contribution, boundaries, and verbosity.

Every pair predeclares the changed construct, affected categories, stable categories and tolerances, expected hard failure, minimum category and total changes, and acceptable alternative interpretations. A target category that is nonapplicable for the passage function is not assigned an artificial decline; that pair instead tests resistance to an irrelevant deduction.

Clustered splitting prevents originals, paraphrases, or derivatives from crossing splits. At least two perturbation realizations are reserved for holdout once a lawful expert corpus exists. Public synthetic analogues exercise the machinery without standing in for the private expert-reviewed corpus.
