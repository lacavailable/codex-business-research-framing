# Lean automated calibration methodology

This study is **automated source-grounded triangulation**, not expert validation. Three cascading roles replace the incomplete eight-role design. Roles A and B run independently; Role C receives their sealed records and adversarially verifies evidence and materiality. Deterministic reconciliation is the default, with targeted model adjudication only for a material dispute.

Applicability is predetermined from passage function, requested task, construction facts, and attempted layers. A model may accept it or challenge it with evidence. Dimension decisions are reported separately for structure fidelity, managerial framing, scholarly positioning, evidence discipline, and prose usability.

Development proceeds through zero-call deterministic checks, four sentinels, eight development cases, and eight validation cases. Each case receives three primary calls and one opaque paired-contrast call. Development and validation each allow at most eight targeted adjudications. Exact calls are checkpointed, and a resource limit pauses rather than fails the study.

Private source wording, spans, packets, and raw records stay ignored. Public artifacts contain schemas, prompts, hashes, counts, aggregate metrics, and candid status only. Neither automated nor expert holdout is opened during development or validation.
